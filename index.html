About Don Samec

Based Cork, Ireland
Education MA Cinematography, ESEC Paris
Experience 10+ years
Artworks documented 1,000+
Artist name Poetonic

Born in California, trained in Paris, living in Cork. A decade of visual production across Europe and the US — then a pivot toward the artists I kept working alongside.

My career began in Corsica and Paris, shooting for production companies, cultural institutions, and local businesses. That grounding — the discipline of framing, sequencing, and editing — runs through everything I do now.

In 2021 I launched Poetonic, my own collage practice, and built the platform to house it from scratch: shop, portfolio, chat feature, all photography and video produced in-house. That site is the most direct proof of what I offer here.

I help artists with the visual infrastructure their work deserves. Not branding strategy, not finding your voice — I come in once you know what you're doing and make sure the world can see it properly.
