Don Samec is a California-born French mixed-media artist, photographer, and creative producer based in Cork, Ireland.

With an MA in Cinematography from E.S.E.C. Paris and over a decade of international experience, he brings technical fluency to artistic practice. Having documented over 1,000 artworks and exhibited globally, he understands the visual rigour required to represent an artist's work in the contemporary era.
